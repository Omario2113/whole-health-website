Equinox

Albany NY

Equinox is a dynamic human services organization in New York's Capital Region. Founded in 1947, Equinox has a dedicated staff of 150 and serves 5,200 people annually in 12 locations, including residential facilities. Equinox serves youth, adults and families whose lives have been impacted by domestic violence, drug and alcohol addictions, mental illness, homelessness and the challenges of living amid poverty and violence.

Crime Victims

Albany NY

Provides a comprehensive range of services to emotionally and physically injured crime victims in Albany County. Services are available to anyone of any age who has experienced a sexual assault or other violent crime at any time in their life, or who has been affected by someone else's assault.

Therapy

Capital District

Therapy is not only for people having a serious relationship problem, experiencing a trauma, or have been feeling depressed or diagnosed with anxiety disorder. Participating in therapy is something you can pursue if you have an area of your life you want to improve to achieve your goals.
